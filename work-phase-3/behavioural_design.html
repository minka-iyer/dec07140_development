Behavioural Design

The focus of this section of the Style Guide is on Behavioural Design, which addresses the user's interactions and experiences with a product or interface. It ensures that the design is intuitive, efficient, and enjoyable to use, ultimately leading to a positive overall experience.
Click on each of the buttons to learn more about their design and functionality.
