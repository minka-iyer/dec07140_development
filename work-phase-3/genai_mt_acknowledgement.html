GenAI & Machine Translation Acknowledgement

Transparency

I used ChatGPT (GPT-5) during the creation of Work Package 3. The tool was primarily used to generate the initial HTML skeletons for content-heavy pages such as Behavioural Design and Ethical Response, and to brainstorm CSS ideas for layout, accessibility, and visual hierarchy. I also used it to refine written sections such as my ethical analysis and client response for conciseness and clarity.

Purposeful Application

My use of GenAI directly supported my learning goals by helping me understand semantic HTML structure, responsive design, and professional documentation style. Using ChatGPT as a design assistant allowed me to approach the project more efficiently, providing a framework I could adapt and expand. This aligned with the task requirements by enhancing both the technical accuracy and readability of my submission.

Critical Engagement

I engaged critically with the AI‚Äôs output rather than accepting it at face value. I frequently rewrote and restructured AI-generated sections to suit my design identity and academic tone. For example, I added my own creative elements such as annotations in the Reflective Design page and reworked any wording that felt too generic or inconsistent with my project voice. I also evaluated GenAI-generated imagery but chose not to use it, as it did not align with my intended aesthetic or brand identity ‚Äî instead, I sourced imagery from Pexels that better reflected my visual direction.

Personal Voice & Ownership

The final website and written content represent my own design thinking, reasoning, and creativity. I designed all original graphics for the Style Guide to express an innovative and cohesive brand identity. My ethical analysis and proposed solutions were entirely my own; ChatGPT was used only for editorial refinement, not idea generation.

Reflection on Learning

Using GenAI tools allowed me to work more iteratively and see alternative ways of structuring both code and written content. It improved my ability to critically assess AI suggestions and decide what aligned with my design intent. I became more confident in identifying when AI adds value versus when to rely on my own creative judgement. A key challenge was maintaining authenticity while using AI efficiently ‚Äî a balance I consciously managed by prioritising my own voice, design intuition, and ideas throughout the project.
