Context and Purpose

I used ChatGPT 5.0 to generate an initial HTML/CSS skeleton for the “Design Implications” section of my ethics analysis. I had a clear creative vision but needed a starting point, so I used ChatGPT as a launch pad.

Critical Decision-making and ownership

I only used the skeleton to create the grid; I designed the rest myself by adjusting colour and formatting, creating the icons, and writing the text.

Accuracy, Bias, and Ethical Awareness

The AI’s suggested colour scheme wasn’t sufficiently readable, so I changed it to a higher-contrast palette aligned with accessibility guidelines and the project style guide.

Privacy and Sensitive Content

Not Applicable in this case.

Reflection on Learning

I wanted to try new ways of expressing my creativity and AI-generated code helped me bring my vision to life. I was still in control of the visual and user experience decisions while Chat GPT gave me an understanding of how to structure the visual elements.

Attribution and Final Statement

I used ChatGPT to support my creative ideas for my website and adapted it where appropriate. All outputs were critically reviewed and integrated, and I maintained control over the creative process and did not use it as a replacement for my own learning.
